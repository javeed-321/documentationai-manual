## Overview

ARMOR DSPM helps organizations discover, understand, and protect   sensitive data across enterprise and AI environments. It pairs data discovery with contextual intelligence, then drives risk-based remediation so security teams focus on the exposures that matter most. Because discovery and classification begin automatically once a repository is connected, teams see value in hours rather than months. See the [Seclore ARMOR DSPM overview](https://www.seclore.com/armor/dspm/) for the product background.

## What makes it different

Conventional DSPM tools stop at discovery. ARMOR goes further by combining discovery with the Semantic Triad and risk-driven remediation, so findings are ranked by business impact rather than raw count.

- **Understands data, not just patterns.** Content, Context, and Intent together.
- **Prioritizes by impact.** Sensitivity combined with Exposure.
- **Acts fast.** Auto-discovery and classification on connection.
