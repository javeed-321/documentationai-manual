## Overview

This page groups ARMOR DSPM capabilities into four areas: discover, classify, assess risk, and protect. Use it as a map of what the platform does and where each capability is documented.

## Capabilities at a glance

| Area | Capability | What it does |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| Discover | Repository connectors | Connect cloud and on-premises stores for automatic discovery |
| Discover | Continuous scanning | Re-scan on change so the catalog stays current |
| Classify | Semantic Triad | Label by Content, Context, and Intent |
| Classify | Category alignment | Roll detected data types into business categories |
| Risk | Sensitivity plus Exposure | Score and rank findings by business impact |
| Risk | Remediation queues | Work Critical and Elevated tiers first |
| Protect | Classification-driven policy | Attach usage controls based on the label |
| Protect | Watermarking and tracking | Trace the origin of a leaked asset |
