## Overview

Watch this short walkthrough to see how ARMOR DSPM connects to a data repository, discovers sensitive assets, and prioritizes risk automatically. The demo follows a single data store from connection to remediation so you can see the full lifecycle in one pass.

## What the demo covers

The walkthrough is organized into four short chapters:

1. Connecting an Amazon S3 bucket as a data source
2. Running the first discovery and classification pass
3. Reading the risk dashboard and the Sensitivity plus Exposure breakdown
4. Applying a classification-driven protection policy

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Watching at 1.25x speed works well for a first pass. Come back to the risk dashboard chapter once you have connected your own repository so the numbers feel familiar.
</Callout>
