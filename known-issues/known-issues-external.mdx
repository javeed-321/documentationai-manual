## Overview

This page lists known issues that may affect your use of ARMOR DSPM, along with the impact and any workaround. It is the customer-facing view; each entry is a confirmed defect with a planned fix.

<Callout kind="info">
  If you hit something not listed here, submit it through the [FAQ Requests Tracker](/faqs/faq-requests-tracker) or your support channel so it can be triaged.
</Callout>

## Current known issues

| ID | Area | What you may see | Workaround |
|----|------|------------------|-----------|
| KI-101 | Warehouse connector | Some columns skipped on very wide tables | Narrow the scan scope per run |
| KI-102 | Dashboard | Tier counts can lag briefly after a scan | Refresh the dashboard |
| KI-108 | Classification | Image-only PDFs are not classified | Add a text layer before scanning |

<Callout kind="info">
  Workarounds are temporary. Each issue has a fix planned; confirm the current status with your support contact.
</Callout>

## What counts as a known issue

A known issue is a confirmed defect with a planned fix. Two related pages help you tell the difference:

- A hard boundary of the platform is a [Technical Limitation](/known-issues/technical-limitation).
- A behavior that is working as designed is [Expected Behaviour](/known-issues/expected-behaviour).
